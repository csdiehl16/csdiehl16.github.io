About me

I am a JavaScript / TypeScript and Python expert, with deep knowledge of data visualization tools including D3.js and Mapbox. My work has won APs global best of the week award three times, and has been longlisted for an Information is Beautiful Award.

Previously, I analyzed data and produced visualizations for major U.S. transit agencies. I hold a masters degree in Urban Planning, with an emphasis on urban informatics, from the University of Washington.
